SARIMA Model Equation

ϕ(B)Φ(Bₘ)(1 - B)ᵈ(1 - Bᵐ)ᴰ Zₜ = θ(B)Θ(Bₘ) aₜ

B is the backshift operator

d and D represent the degree of differencing for non-seasonal and seasonal components, respectively

p and P are the orders of autoregressive (AR) components for non-seasonal and seasonal terms

q and Q are the orders of moving average (MA) components for non-seasonal and seasonal terms

s denotes the seasonal period

Zₜ represents the observed time series

aₜ is a white noise error term
